About

The Sri Chinmoy Centre provides an opportunity for students of Sri Chinmoy to meditate and share the practical philosophy of working towards a more harmonious and peaceful world. The first Sri Chinmoy Centre, was established in 1966, in Puerto Rico. There are now centres in over 60 countries in all corners of the world. Read More

Our Members

Members of the Sri Chinmoy Centre come from a diversity of backgrounds but share a common interest in meditation and spirituality. Inspired by the teachings and example of Sri Chinmoy, they offer a variety of spiritual, cultural, musical, athletic and humanitarian events in their local towns and cities. Events organised by the Sri Chinmoy Centre

 

Free Meditation classes

Through our public meditation classes, a spiritual approach to life is made freely available to seekers of truth and inner peace all over the world. At Sri Chinmoy’s own personal request, all meditation classes and public concerts are given without charge, in the conviction that the inner fulfilment meditation can bring is the unequivocal birthright of every individual. Find out more...

 

Athletic Events

Sri Chinmoy has long been a great believer in the power of sport to bring people together, and to provide a channel through which athletes can find the very best within themselves. The Sri Chinmoy Marathon Team, the sporting organisation founded by Sri Chinmoy in 1977, organises fun-runs, marathons, ultra distance races and triathlons in over 20 countries around the world. Read more...

 

 

Concerts of Meditative Music

Music is an indispensable part of the life of the Sri Chinmoy Centre, and we frequently organise concerts of soulful music for the general public in many locations around the world, read more...

 

Initiatives for a Better World

All around the world, volunteers from the Sri Chinmoy Centre coordinate the following initiatives as part of their contribution towards a more harmonious and equitable world:

World Harmony Run

The World Harmony Run is a grassroots initiative in which runners carry a burning torch through over 70 countries and pass it from hand to hand in a gesture of friendship and global solidarity.

Read more...

 

The Oneness-Heart Tears and Smiles humanitarian initiative.

The Oneness-Heart Tears and Smiles initiative offers humanitarian aid services in 24 countries, and is entirely staffed by volunteer members of the Sri Chinmoy Centre. As the name suggests, the organisation's activities are guided by the principle of oneness between the volunteers and the people they are serving, and overcoming the problem of superiority and inferiority between donor and recipient that might otherwise exist. Read more...
