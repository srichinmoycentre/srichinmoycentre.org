Daily Jharna Kala Artwork

From 1975 to his passing in 2007, Sri Chinmoy painted over 140,000 paintings which he called Jharna-Kala, which means fountain-art in his native Bengali. In addition, he drew over 16 million birds symbolising the freedom of the human soul. Find out more on srichinmoyart.com...

Peace Run - latest updates

Founded by Sri Chinmoy in 1987, the Peace Run runs in over 100 countries, where local communities along the route can get involved, hold the torch and add their heartfelt wishes for a better world to the millions of others who have held the torch before them. Find out more on peacerun.org

From 2004-2013 the Run was also known as the World Harmony Run. During 2013, the listings below will display the latest updates from both peacerun.org and worldharmonyrun.org.
